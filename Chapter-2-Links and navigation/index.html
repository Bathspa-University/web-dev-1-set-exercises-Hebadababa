Dark



    

        

            

                
Plot

                

                    "Dark" is a German science fiction thriller streaming television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. Set in the fictional German town of Winden, the series follows the intertwined destinies of four families as they uncover a time travel conspiracy which spans several generations.

Characters

            

                Jonas Kahnwald: The central character, who discovers the secrets of Winden and its time travel phenomena.
            

            

                Ulrich Nielsen: A police officer who investigates the disappearance of children in Winden.
            

            

                Charlotte Doppler: The head of Winden's police force, who uncovers the truth behind the mysterious events.
            

            

                Katharina Nielsen: Ulrich's wife, who becomes entangled in the time travel mystery.
            

            

                The Stranger: A mysterious figure with knowledge of the time loops in Winden.
            

            

                Eva: A key player in the time travel conspiracy.
